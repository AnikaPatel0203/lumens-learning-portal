Grade 5 Hindi

Hindi is the official language of India, rooted in Sanskrit and enriched by Persian, Arabic, and other influences.
As an academic subject, it offers a gateway to India's rich literature, culture, and history through works by renowned poets and writers.
It is also crucial for national unity, providing a common platform for communication and a link to cultural heritage.
