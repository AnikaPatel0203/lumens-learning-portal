Grade 5 Gujarati

Gujarati is a Western Indo-Aryan language spoken by over 62 million people,
Making it an official language in the Indian state of Gujarat and a recognized minority language in South Africa.
The language is written in its own script, which differs from Devanagari, and has a rich history that is divided into three periods: Old, Medieval, and Modern Gujarati.
It is an important cultural language, the language of Mahatma Gandhi, and has a diverse literary tradition that includes poetry, prose, and more.
