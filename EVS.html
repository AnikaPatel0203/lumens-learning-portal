Grade 5 EVS

Environmental Studies (EVS) is an interdisciplinary subject that studies the relationship between humans and their surrondings.
It covers a wide range of topics including natural resources like air and water, biodiversity, pollution, and climate change.
The goal of EVS is to understand our environment and to promote responsible
Practices for its preservation, like reducing waste, conserving resources, and protecting ecosystems.
